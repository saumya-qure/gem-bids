Setup (one-time)
  1. Run the proxy on your machine: python3 gem_proxy.py
  2. Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:
    GeM → Proxy
  3. Open bidplus.gem.gov.in/all-bids, log in if needed, then click the bookmarklet. You should see a green toast.
  4. Come back here — the dot in the top right should turn green. Tokens last a few hours; re-click the bookmarklet when they expire.
